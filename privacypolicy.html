Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Campus Cinema uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Campus Cinema uses cookies to help Campus Cinema identify and track visitors, their usage of http://www.campuscinema.live, and their website access preferences. Campus Cinema visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Campus Cinema's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Campus Cinema's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Campus Cinema's use of cookies.
